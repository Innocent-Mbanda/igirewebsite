ACADEMY OF WOMEN ENTREPRENUERSHIP-RWANDA

ABOUT


Academy of Women Entrepreneur (AWE) is an international program to teach women around the world to be successful entrepreneurs. There are specific challenges that women face as business owners from gender inequality, limited funding, balancing responsibilities, inadequate support, limited knowledge, unfavorable business environment, and timidity. These are the root why so many ran businesses fail. Research has shown countries that have strong presence of women entrepreneurs are stronger economically in addition to being more peaceful. Our goal is to empower women to enable them be more successful as entrepreneurs.In this 12-week program participants will refine their business through an online course Dreambuilder, as well as in-person workshops
